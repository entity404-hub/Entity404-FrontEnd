World's Favourite Trek
Marlin 7

About

Engineered for riders who crave performance without breaking the bank, the Marlin 7 features a lightweight Alpha Silver Aluminum frame and a RockShox Judy suspension fork that delivers smooth handling on rugged trails and urban commutes alike. Its cross-country geometry draws from Trek’s higher-end models, offering a responsive ride that’s ideal for beginners and seasoned cyclists looking for a reliable hardtail. Whether you're tackling light off-road terrain, bikepacking, or daily errands, the Marlin 7’s smart component mix and sleek styling make it a top choice for riders who want more from their first MTB.
